Posting to the List

We welcome posts from others to our list. You do not have to be subscribed to post to our list. To post to our list, send your message to acmx@mailman.mines.edu.

Note that there only a small group of people who may post to our list without moderation (officers and faculty advisors). When you post, a message will automatically be sent to the list administrator asking for their approval.

We ask that you follow good email etiquette when posting to our list. Sending HTML mail or TOFU mail is a great way to get your post rejected.
